Aquapon Food for the Future

a system of aquaculture in which the waste produced by farmed fish or other aquatic animals supplies nutrients for plants grown hydroponically, which in turn purify the water.

Aquaponics is here to supply a large number of people with fresh organic foods for less money.

Here at aquaponics, we pride ourselves on our efficiency and dedication.
